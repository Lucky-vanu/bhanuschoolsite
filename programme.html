Junior Programme

Joyfully explore the world, ask questions, build basic skills.

Themes to sense the world – Observe and experience the world through exciting themes like family, festivals, nature etc. that bring together many subjects.

Communicate – Build a basic vocabulary along with listening and speaking skills, listen to and tell stories, write freely and read with interest.

Analyze – Understand the concept of numbers and the skill to apply it to basic mathematical operations, counting, classifying and sequencing objects.

Nurturing Mind, Body and Spirit – Strengthen a growing body, establish relationships and embrace learning.

Middle Programme

Master Concepts – Understand deeply the ‘what, why, how’ of basic concepts in science, language, mathematics and social studies.

Projects to Apply Knowledge – To create practical projects – publish a magazine, build a machine, tend a garden.

Discover a Talent – Deepen watch child’s interest in one or more areas like sports or music or literature.

Confident and Connected – Handle unknown situations, express feelings, work in teams and make decisions.

Senior Programme

Solve Problems – Understand higher-order concepts and use proof, reasoning and research to find solutions.

Express Creatively – Master writing and speaking, images and music to create original work.

Connect with experts – Exposure to and interaction with professionals (doctors, musicians, scientists etc.) to understand how learning connects to the real world and leads to a career of choice.

Prepare for Boards, Career and Life – Self-study habits, test-taking skills, individual motivation to succeed in examinations and life goals.
